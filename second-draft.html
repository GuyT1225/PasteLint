Why this rewrite works

    What SecondDraft does

    SecondDraft takes text that feels overly polished, repetitive, vague, or robotic and rewrites it into something clearer and more natural.

    Why this is different

    Most AI humanizer tools either barely change the text or change it too much. SecondDraft aims for the useful middle: clean, natural wording without fake typos, strange phrasing, or meaning drift.

    Why some AI writing feels off

    AI writing doesn’t just feel off because of word choice. It’s often about patterns — predictable phrasing, repetitive structure, and flat sentence rhythm.

    SecondDraft focuses on what readers actually notice: cleaner rhythm, less filler, simpler phrasing, and better flow.

    It is not built to bypass AI detection. It is built to make rough drafts easier to review, edit, and use.
